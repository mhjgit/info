Thesis Projects

Most of the projects below can form the basis for a Master of Science thesis or a longer PhD thesis. The topics reflect my current research interests. All project descriptions are linked as PDFs.

  1. Machine learning for finance: from Physics Informed Neural Networks to Parametric Matrix Models
  2. Machine learning for time-dependent quantum mechanical problems: from TDHF to MCTDHF and time-dependent Coupled-Cluster theory
  3. Machine learning for quantum computing
  4. Quantum computing and unitary Coupled-Cluster theory
  5. Simulations of spin qubit systems and entanglement in many-body systems
  6. Implementation of Physics-informed algorithms on quantum computers
  7. Quantum reinforcement learning applied to quantum sensors (description coming soon)
