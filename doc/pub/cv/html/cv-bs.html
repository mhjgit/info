Brief research overview

I am a theoretical physicist with a strong interest in computational physics, computational science and many-body theory in general, and the nuclear many-body problem and nuclear structure problems in particular. This means that I study various methods for solving either Schrödinger's equation or Dirac's equation for many interacting particles, spanning from algorithmic aspects to the mathematical properties of such methods, including machine learning and quantum computing.
