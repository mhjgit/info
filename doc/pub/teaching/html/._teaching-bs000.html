Thesis projects

Most of the projects listed below can form the basis for a Master of Science thesis or a longer PhD thesis. The topics listed below reflect to a large extent my own interests and research topics. Below you will also find links to actual thesis projects.

  1. Machine learning for finance, from Physics Informed Neural networks to Parametric Matrix Models
  2. Machine learning for time-dependent quantum mechanical problems, from time-dependent Hartree-Fock theory to Multiconfiguration time-dependent Hartree-Fock and time-dependent Coupled-Cluster theory
  3. Machine learning for quantum computing
  4. Quantum computing and unitary Coupled-Cluster theory
  5. Simulations of spin qubit systems and entanglement in many-body systems
  6. Implementation of Physics-informed algorithms on quantum computers
  7. Quantum reinformcebemt learning applied to quantum sensors, to be added
